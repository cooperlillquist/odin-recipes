Chicken Caesar Saland

Recipe Description

This crisp, fresh caesar salad and perfectly juicy grilled chicken (hello, Spring!) has been getting me through these past few weeks.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons lemon zest
  • 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 heads of romaine lettuce
  • caesar salad dressing
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated parmesean
  • 1 bag of crutons

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, combine olive oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, garlic, 1 teaspoon salt and 1/2 teaspoon pepper.
  2. In a gallon size Ziploc bag or large bowl, combine chicken and olive oil mixture; marinate for at least 2 hours, turning the bag occasionally. Drain the chicken from the marinade.
  3. Add chicken to grill, and cook, turning occasionally, until chicken is completely cooked through, reaching an internal temperature of 165 degrees F, about 4-5 minutes per side. Let cool before dicing into bite-size pieces.
  4. To assemble the salad, place romaine in a large bowl; top with chicken and croutons. Pour the dressing on top of the salad and gently toss to combine. Top with Parmesan.
