Asian Stir Fry

Recipe Description

This is it! This is the recipe that gets me through the week! I love how simple and easy it is to cook and how delicious it tastes. There are so many variations to this recipe that it is perfect to make with a family.

This past month I have been doing some meal prep and I decided to try this as well. I cooked it up on Sunday and added it into 5 days of lunches for me. It was simple to warm up and was filling all day.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Vegetables: Red bell pepper, yellow bell pepper, sugar snap peas, carrots, broccoli
  • Sauce: soy sauce, garlic (minced), brown sugar, sesame oil, chicken broth cornstarch
  • Garnish:green onion, sesame seeds

Steps

  1. In a wok or large skillet add 1 Tablespoon olive oil over medium high heat. Add bell pepper, peas, carrots, mushrooms, and broccoli. Saut√© 2-3 minutes until veggies are almost tender.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, garlic, brown sugar, sesame oil, chicken broth, and cornstarch.
  3. Pour sauces over veggies and cook unitl the sauce has thickend
  4. Garnish with chopped green onion and sesame seeds
